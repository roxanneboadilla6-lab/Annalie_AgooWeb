About Agoo

Agoo is one of the oldest towns in La Union known for its rich history, religious heritage, and beautiful coastal attractions.

Mission

To promote Agoo as a sustainable tourism destination while preserving its culture and environment.

Vision

To become a leading tourism hub in La Union known for heritage and hospitality.

Values

Faith, Unity, Culture, Sustainability
